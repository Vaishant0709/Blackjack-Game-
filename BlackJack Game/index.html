Black Jack Game

Black Jack Rules
Blackjack (or 21) is a card game where you compete against the dealer to get a hand closest to 21 points, without going over.
  • Card Values:
    • Number cards (2-10): Face value
    • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King): 10 points
    • Ace: 1 point or 11 points (if over 21 then can be used as 1)
  • Goal: Get a hand total closer to 21 than the dealer without exceeding 21 (busting).
  • Gameplay:
    • Player receives two cards face up.
    • Dealer receives two cards, one face up and one face down (the "hole card").
    • Player decides to "hit" (get another card) or "stand" (stay with current hand).
    • Player keeps hitting or standing until satisfied or busts.
    • Dealer follows predetermined rules based on their hand.
    • The hand closer to 21 wins!
  • Winning:
    • Blackjack (natural): Player gets an Ace and a 10-value card for a starting total of 21.
    • Regular win: Player's hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without busting.
    • Push (tie): Player and dealer have the same hand total.
    • Loss: Player busts or dealer has a higher hand without busting.
